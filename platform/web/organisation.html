What is being built here

The engine already computes every one of these for a company. The screens are what is missing.

  • The company tax calendar — PAYE, VAT, WHT and NSSF on the 15th; provisional tax on the last day of the 6th and 12th month of your year of income. Computed from the Act, not typed into a spreadsheet once and forgotten.
  • The director trap — a director who has never filed a personal return blocks your company’s tax clearance certificate. It is invisible from inside your own books, and you find out on the day you lose the tender.
  • TCC readiness — the five criteria URA actually applies, four of which are administrative and therefore objectionable.
  • The WHT credit tracker — the 6% withheld from your invoices is not a cost. It is a prepayment of your tax, and most Ugandan businesses never claim it.
