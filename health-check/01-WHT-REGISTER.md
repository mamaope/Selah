**The single most valuable spreadsheet a Ugandan consultant will ever keep.**

*Selah · v1 · Verified 11 July 2026 · ITA Cap 338 s.128 (credit) · s.136 (the 6% on goods and services)*

---

## 🔑 Why this exists

**Withholding tax deducted from you is NOT a cost. It is PREPAID TAX.**

It is a **credit** against your income tax liability — you have already paid it. But the credit is only claimable **if you hold the certificate.**

> **A credit you cannot evidence is a credit you do not have.**

No certificate → no credit → **you pay tax twice on the same income.**

Most Ugandan consultants have never collected most of their certificates. This is not a marginal problem. It is a systematic, silent, five-figure transfer from small consultants to the state, every year, entirely by accident.

**Isaac invoiced UGX 60,000,000 over five years. 6% was withheld: UGX 3,600,000. He held 4 certificates out of 11.**

## The three things that surprise people

**1. It survives the presumptive regime.**
Even a small business paying **presumptive tax** — where no deductions of any kind are allowed — **keeps its WHT credit.** Schedule 3, Part II. The certificate holds its value in *every* regime.

**2. It reduces your provisional tax.**
Provisional tax is *(50% × estimated annual tax) **less WHT already withheld from you***. Businesses routinely pay provisional tax **in full** *and* suffer WHT — **paying the same tax twice, in the same year.**

**3. The withholding agent is personally liable.**
If your client failed to withhold, URA does not chase you. **It chases them.** That is a useful thing to know when you are asking them for a certificate they never issued.

## Housekeeping

- **Keep it for 5 years minimum** (TPCA). Design for 7.
- **A scan is a record. A promise is not.** Photograph every certificate the day it arrives.
- **Reconcile monthly, not annually.** A client who cannot find a certificate from last month will certainly not find one from three years ago.
- **The certificate is the asset.** The invoice is just paper about it.
